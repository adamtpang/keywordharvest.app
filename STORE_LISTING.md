## Extension Name
Keyword Harvest

## Short Description (132 char max)
Find and extract all keyword matches from any webpage with context, export results, and highlight matches on the page.

## Detailed Description

**Keyword Harvest** makes it easy to find, extract, and manage keyword matches across any webpage.

### Key Features:
- **Smart Search**: Find all instances of any keyword or phrase on the current page
- **Context View**: See each match with surrounding text for better understanding
- **Export Options**: Download results as TXT or JSON files
- **Page Highlighting**: Visually highlight all matches directly on the webpage
- **Advanced Options**: Case-sensitive search and regex pattern support
- **Quick Actions**: Copy all results to clipboard with one click
- **Search History**: Keep track of your last 5 searches
- **Keyboard Shortcut**: Press Ctrl+Shift+E (Cmd+Shift+E on Mac) to quickly open

### Perfect For:
- Content writers analyzing keyword usage
- Researchers extracting specific terms from documents
- SEO professionals auditing web content
- Students studying text patterns
- Anyone who needs to find and extract text efficiently

### How to Use:
1. Click the extension icon or press Ctrl+Shift+E
2. Enter your keyword or phrase
3. Click "Extract" to find all matches
4. View results with context and location
5. Export, copy, or highlight as needed

### Privacy:
- No data sent to external servers
- All processing happens locally in your browser
- Search history stored locally only

Simple, fast, and powerful keyword extraction at your fingertips!
